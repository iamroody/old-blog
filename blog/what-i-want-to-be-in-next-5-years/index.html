What I Want to Be in Next 5 Years ?

Nov 6th, 2011

Since the first day i entered into company, i always ask myself this question, The process of thinking makes me be aware of what and why i am doing this currently.

For me, different stages have different answers even though i just worked here for 4 months. In the first 2 months, i just thought about how to survive in TW, and avoid being fired in 6 months’ probation period. I want to write clean code ,learn more new things about software development, pursue high quality software, but I don’t have clear career plan. During TWU, i began to know what i should do and how i prepare for that.

From a thoughtworker’s blog, i know that one person’s future depends on fit that you between your company’s core values. Person’s development is related to your company’s development. What’s your company’s core offering ? What can you offer values in those offerings?

The following picture is thoughtworks’ core offering:

thoughtworks’ core values are software excellence, Methodology around it is continuous design and delivery . the left side is consulting, including experience design, continuous improvement and technical skills. The right side is ALM , including Go, Mingle and Twist.

According to core offerings, i make a simple plan for myself:

In the first 2 years , i will focus on continuous design and delivery, learn the enough technical skills to handle with daily work easily, contribute velocity , dive into some special tech areas which i am interested or more promising, for example , i am interested in UX and DEVOPS. I prefer to work in offshore delivery project. It will great that if the client just like REA that so cooperative and kind. I hope have enough business trip to practice myself communicating with clients.

In the third or fourth year, i hope try some consulting task and have a specify technical that i am good at. I prefer do more BA’s work at that period and hope have a chance to participate in a project from begin to end. If i have a chance i want to more work on tw’s studio.

In the fifth year, i hope i can still work in thoughtworks. At that time , i hope i can be good at consulting work , give clients awesome technical advice, be a tech leader in a project.

I hope everyday i can be better than yesterday !

Reference: 我的未来在哪里？ http://www.tuzei8.com/page/3/
