The Next 30 Days

Nov 8th, 2011

Michael shared a TED video named “the next 30 days”, it talks about make some personal plan in next 30 days.

Because whether you like, the following days will go away, so why not do some thing that you really want to do but never done. In next 30 days, try to insist on it every day . In this way, the next 30 days become more meaningful for you ,and worth memory.

After that video, i decided write blog every day in next 30 days.

It really works well, i wrote about 5 blogs at first way. But last night i forgot to write it, why? i worked on pair stairs until 3:00 AM. But i still should write it in the morning , not delayed it to the last minutes.

So, what’s your plan for next 30 days ?
