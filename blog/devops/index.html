DEVOPS

Nov 10th, 2011 | Comments

Today, Cosmin gave us a session about devops, but the session didn’t tell too much about what’s the devops, it focused on another aspect about project. When the project’s development is finished, who will care about the rest works, including how to deploy , maintain, upgrade, monitor and so on. The session talked a lot about what the operation team need and do.

You can find the definition about devops on wikipedia, but the best way to learn devops is practice， try to do some devops things on your local machine, for example chef, pupet, build pipeline, one click deploy and so on .

I am really interested in devops, i will start to learn from now no.
